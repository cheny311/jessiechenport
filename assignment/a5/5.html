This is an interactive optical illusion that uses face tracking technology.

When your face is detected by the webcam, the text "Can you see me?" becomes scrambled and distorted with a blurry overlay effect.

When no face is detected, the text appears clear and readable.

How to use: Allow webcam access when prompted, then move your face in and out of the camera view to see the effect!
